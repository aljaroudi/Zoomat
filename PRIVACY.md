# Privacy Policy for Zoomat

**Last updated: November 11, 2025**

## Data Collection

Zoomat does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information remains exclusively on your device.

## Data Storage

- All event, contact, and check-in data is stored locally using SwiftData
- No third-party services or analytics are used

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions only when needed:

- **Camera**: Required for QR code scanning at check-in
- **Contacts**: Optional, only if you choose to import contacts

## Data Security

Your data never leaves your device.

## Data Deletion

You can delete any data at any time by removing contacts, events, or invitations within the app. To completely remove all data, simply delete the app from your device.
